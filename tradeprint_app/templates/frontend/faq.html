Common Questions

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely happy with your order. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we will gladly accept returns for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also view your order status in your account dashboard.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship globally! Shipping rates and times vary by country. Please proceed to checkout to see specific options for your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay.
